My Hobby - Magic the Gathering

One of my current favorite hobbies is playing Magic: the Gathering. Magic is a 1993 card game made by Richard Garfield PhD, while there are many ways to play the most common format involves two players with 60 card decks attempting to reduce the other player's life total from twenty to zero.
